About me

I am a PhD student in the school of computer science at University of Birmingham, United Kingdom, and a lecturer at University of Bahrain, Bahrain. My research focus on offensive security across different platforms: Computers, Mobiles (specially Android OS) and web browsers. I discovered and reported several security vulnerabilities that affect products belong to Google, Samsung, Huawei and Opera. I obtained my Msc in advanced computer science at University of Birmingham in December 2017.
